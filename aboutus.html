About us

Welcome to Ettan's, a culinary haven founded in 2024 by four visionary chefs: Joni, Vilho, Lauri, and Jani, with Henri leading the charge. United by their passion for crafting exquisite pizzas, they embarked on a journey to redefine the pizza experience.

Joni, the master of flavors, brings a rich palette of tastes to each creation. Vilho, the artisan of the oven, ensures every crust achieves the perfect balance of crispiness and chewiness. Lauri, the maestro of toppings, orchestrates a symphony of fresh, locally sourced ingredients, creating vibrant and savory compositions. Jani, the sauce sorcerer, infuses each pizza with a secret blend of sauces that dance on the taste buds.

Guided by Henri, their collective ambition is to transport you to a world where each bite is an adventure. Ettan's is not just a pizzeria; it's a celebration of culinary artistry, a place where tradition meets innovation, and every pizza tells a story.
